Education

The high quality educational institutions in Pune attract thousands of students from across India and even abroad every year. ... The city has a number of other deemed varsities and private institutions of higher education that are extremely popular among Indian as well as international students.

Sinhgad Fort

Natural Heritage

As Punekars, we are extremely lucky to have the Sahyadri range surrounding us. Apart from immense natural beauty, the Sahyadris is home to a number of forts that make for perfect weekend trekking destinations. Not only are these treks a fun and exciting way to keep up with our fitness regimes, but they also treat us to stunning views AND let us go back in time as we explore these magnificent old structures.
